3/1/22: Bing Bong

This is Bing Bong. Bing Bong is a great cat. He is wearing his favorite shark hat. Bing Bong's favorite pasttime is imagining he is a shark. He likes to bite his owners, because he thinks he is a shark. Bing Bong was voted "Cutest Shark of All Time" by the SACA (Shark and Cat Association) in 2022. 10/10. Good Job Bing Bong.

3/2/22: Noodle

We love Noodle. Noodle is perfect. Noodle is always trying her best. She gets scared a lot, so the term "scaredy cat" was actually named after Noodle. Funny enough, Noodle absolutely hates eating noodles. Her favorite food is cheese. Absolutely 10/10.
